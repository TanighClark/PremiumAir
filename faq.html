FAQ

For more cosmic inquiries, reach out to our customer service team via the contact form on our website, or send a message to cosmicqueries@premiumair.space. We're always here to help!

What exactly is Premium Air?

Premium Air is a whimsical concept born from the fusion of satire and creativity, offering "cosmically sourced" air for your vehicle's tires. It's a playful nod to the extravagance of luxury products, serving as a reminder not to take life too seriously. So, while you can't actually purchase air from the edge of the universe for your tires, we hope the idea brings a smile to your face and a light-hearted moment to your day. 

Remember, Premium Air is as real as unicorns dancing on rainbows – a fun imaginary product on a website dedicated to tickling your funny bone!

How do you source your cosmic air?

With a dedicated team of space divers and cosmic collectors, we venture to the edge of Earth's atmosphere (and sometimes a tad beyond) to harvest the finest, most ethereal air the cosmos has to offer.

Do you offer international (or interplanetary) shipping?

Yes, we ship globally and to select space stations. For interplanetary orders, please allow for additional shipping time due to orbital dynamics.
